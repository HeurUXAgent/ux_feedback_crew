**Priority:** MEDIUM

**Why it matters:** Without appropriate descriptive alternative text, screen reader users cannot understand the content or purpose of product images and other meaningful non-text content. **What to do:**
- Implement programmatic `alt` attributes for all product image components, dynamically populating them with concise, descriptive text (e.g., 'Blue men's shirt', 'Toddler kids' outfit set', 'Black puffer jacket'). The alt text should convey the essential information or function of the image.
- Review all other non-text content (e.g., decorative icons, promotional banners that convey information visually) to ensure they either have appropriate alt text or are marked as decorative if they add no informational value.

**Wireframe changes:** Annotate all image components on wireframes with a note indicating the requirement for dynamic, descriptive `alt` text. This is primarily a code-level implementation but should be a standard requirement in design documentation.
